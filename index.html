Welcome to StethoSmart - Real-Time Heart Sound

StethoSmart is a digital stethoscope project designed to turn your phone into a regular stethoscope. Our app relays real-time heart sounds, displays a phonocardiograph, and uses an ML model to classify heart sounds. The platform also enables telemedicine by allowing you to send heart sounds to healthcare professionals for further analysis and support. Stay connected and make healthcare more accessible!
